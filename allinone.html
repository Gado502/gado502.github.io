About Me

Education

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Adama Science and Technology University, where I specialized in Web Technologies, UI/UX Design, and Cloud Computing. Prior to this, I earned a Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems from Haramaya University, which laid the foundation for my expertise in computer science and information management. Together, these academic experiences have equipped me with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to excel in technology-driven fields.

Experience

With 5+ years of experience in web development, I have also worked as a System Administrator, Project Manager, and Graphic Designer, supporting both startups and large organizations. I have delivered trainings as a university lecturer and trainer, focusing on AI, project management, and digital tools. Currently, I lead AgriConnect Solutions, a startup building agricultural technology solutions, while engaging in faith-based teaching and community service.

Passion

I am passionate about creating intuitive, beautiful, and accessible user interfaces. I enjoy using modern technologies to bring innovative designs to life. My goal is to enhance user experiences through thoughtful, user-centered design.
